How To Play

  • Buy a Town Hall first to anchor your village.
  • Gold Mines and Elixir Collectors generate resources over time.
  • Archer Towers and Wizard Towers defend your side of the lane.
  • Barracks are required before you can train troops.
  • Hero Hall is required before you can train heroes.
  • Deploy enough troops to destroy the enemy Town Hall.
